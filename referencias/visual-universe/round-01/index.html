Top 8 shortlist for Round 2 deep dive
  1. linear.app Directly in our register: “teams and agents” framing mirrors “profile + agentic AI”. Study their agent-activity mockups, spacing, and how they balance dark palette with a single indigo accent.
  2. cursor.com Status-card grid (“In Progress”, “Ready for Review”) is a direct steal for “agents doing work on my behalf”. Extract hero composition, card motion, and gradient treatment.
  3. emilkowal.ski Gold standard for “animation as proof”. Every section is a live mini-demo, not a screenshot. We want that pattern for our skills sections.
  4. brittanychiang.com Calmest benchmark for a personal dev-designer site: cursor spotlight, dark navy palette, tight content. Useful floor to compare our ambitions against.
  5. rauno.me Masterclass in “quiet density” — lots of content, no visual noise. Study type hierarchy, labels, and how work is indexed without screenshots.
  6. huncwot.com Their tagline (“crafting digital products that AI can only dream of”) is the provocation we’re answering. Study copy hierarchy, case-study pacing, and their aggressive accent color choices.
  7. anthropic.com Warm editorial tone is the closest match for our “serio + cálido” brand. Study spacing rhythm, muted palette, and how they avoid the cold-clinical AI trap.
  8. landonorris.com Neon-lime on near-black is the boldest accent option on our list. Test whether that kind of saturated accent coexists with editorial warmth, or forces us to choose.
