# Senior Product Designer Technical Assessment Questions
**Version 3.0**

## SECTION 1: PRODUCT THINKING
* **Question 1:** Your product's conversion rate drops by 15% over the last quarter. Walk me through your first 48 hours.
* **Question 2:** How do you distinguish a UX problem from a business problem?
* **Question 3:** Tell me about a product decision that improved a business outcome. How did you measure success?
* **Question 4:** What is the difference between an output and an outcome? Give a real example.
* **Question 5:** What makes a product successful?

## SECTION 2: PRODUCT STRATEGY
* **Question 6:** How do you define an MVP?
* **Question 7:** What is NOT an MVP?
* **Question 8:** Your CEO asks for ten new features. How do you decide what gets built?
* **Question 9:** How do you prioritize competing stakeholder requests?
* **Question 10:** What prioritization frameworks have you used? How?

## SECTION 3: DISCOVERY
* **Question 11:** What is Product Discovery?
* **Question 12:** Describe your discovery process from start to finish.
* **Question 13:** A stakeholder requests a new feature. What happens next?
* **Question 14:** How do you validate assumptions?
* **Question 15:** How do you know when you have enough evidence to move forward?

## SECTION 4: USER RESEARCH
* **Question 16:** When should you run user interviews?
* **Question 17:** When should you NOT run user interviews?
* **Question 18:** Tell me about a research project you conducted.
* **Question 19:** How many users are enough for usability testing? Why?
* **Question 20:** What are the biggest mistakes researchers make?

## SECTION 5: UX METRICS
* **Question 21:** What UX metrics do you track most often?
* **Question 22:** Which is more important: Task Success Rate or Time on Task? Why?
* **Question 23:** You have Version A (90% Success Rate, 120 sec Time on Task, 4.8/5 CSAT) and Version B (95% Success Rate, 90 sec Time on Task, 3.1/5 CSAT). Which would you choose?
* **Question 24:** Explain SUS.
* **Question 25:** Explain the HEART framework.

## SECTION 6: DESIGN SYSTEMS
* **Question 26:** What is a Design System?

* **Question 48:** What parts should never be automated?

## SECTION 11: INTERVIEW PERFORMANCE
* **Question 49:** Tell me about a design decision you strongly disagreed with and how you handled it.
* **Question 50:** Why should we hire you instead of another Senior Product Designer?

## SECTION 12: CLAUDE CODE & CURSOR
* **Question 51:** How would you use Claude Code in a real product design workflow without outsourcing your judgment?
* **Question 52:** What kinds of tasks would you give to Cursor versus Claude Code?
* **Question 53:** Describe a design-to-code workflow where AI helps you move from Figma or a product idea to a working prototype.
* **Question 54:** How do you review AI-generated code or UI before trusting it?
* **Question 55:** What risks appear when designers rely too heavily on AI coding tools?

## SECTION 13: FIGMA MCP & AI AGENTS

* **Question 59:** What should never be automated in a Figma-to-code workflow?
* **Question 60:** How would you explain AI agents to a product team that has never used them?
