How to pick

The call I'd make

For a first Tier-3 v3 landing you don't need all ten. I'd pick three with distinct roles:

  1. Piece 05 · Iridescent Craft Sphere — hero anchor, carries "craft" metaphor, most likely to get the "wow".
  2. Piece 03 · Single-Source Prism — architecture block, tells your repo-as-system story without needing copy.
  3. Piece 04 · Agent Swarm Orbit — method section, literalises "profile + agentic AI = combination" as motion.

Piece 01 (Recursive Score Dial), Piece 02 (Data-Strata), Piece 07 (Brushed-Steel Nameplate) are great Tier-2 additions you can layer in next. Pieces 06, 08, 09, 10 are maximum-effort flourishes — save for v4 if you want.
