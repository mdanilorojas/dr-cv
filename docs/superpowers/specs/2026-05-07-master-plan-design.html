01 · Problem

The 2022–2026 narrative gap.

Danilo's public professional narrative freezes in 2022 as "UX/UI Product Designer." Between 2023 and 2026 he has:

  • Shipped production work at Booz Allen Hamilton (Trusted Environments / Developer Portal) — two parallel design systems (/te-skin as an agent-consumable slash-command skill, TE Black as a dark-first DS with per-token WCAG audit trail) and a SharePoint-deployable single-file SPA landing.
  • Built EnRegla, his own compliance SaaS, from zero to a 1.0-MVP in production in 40 days, now with paying pilots.
  • Built Life Update Mobile, a personal tracking product that uses the Gemini API in runtime for semantic analysis.
  • Industrialized an agentic workflow (Claude Code + MCP servers + subagents + skills-lock + recursive numeric review) that multiplies his delivery speed without sacrificing craft.

None of this is visible in any public CV, portfolio or web presence. Clients and recruiters looking him up today see a 2022 UX/UI designer, not the Agentic Designer he is now.

He is currently employed by BairesDev (placed at Booz Allen Hamilton) and is running parallel outreach via LinkedIn to find direct clients.

02 · Goal

A system that keeps itself alive.

A single-source-of-truth repository of Danilo's professional identity (skills, experience, cases, clients, testimonials, network) from which multiple deliverables are generated automatically: skills sheet, CVs, landing page, and eventually a "me" agent.

The platform itself is proof of the thesis: an Agentic Designer who builds the tools agents use to help him ship.

Framing

Profile + agentic AI = combination. Never Danilo as solo superhuman. Honesty as brand value.

03 · Non-goals

  • NOT a rewrite of the 2022 CV. This is a reposition and a narrative closure.
  • NOT a single-channel strategy. Same data, four audiences (US enterprise, LATAM SMB, full-time remote, SaaS investor) plus BairesDev template.
  • NOT a Figma-first workflow. Source of truth is code-first (YAML/JSON/MD in Git); Figma is downstream.
  • NOT mobile-only. Skills sheet & CVs are A4 print-first; landing is mobile-first responsive.
  • NOT a solo-genius narrative. The combination is the product.

04 · Architecture

Single source of truth · agents · generators.

All professional identity lives in /data/. Agents update it; generators emit the deliverables.

dr-cv/
├── data/
│   ├── identity.yaml          # name, role, contact, live status
│   ├── positioning.yaml       # headline variants, taglines
│   ├── skills.yaml            # dual taxonomy (by-layer + by-outcome)
│   ├── experience.yaml        # employment + client history
│   ├── clients.yaml           # enterprise list
│   ├── cases/                 # one .md per case study
│   ├── testimonials/
│   │   ├── verified.yaml      # real (Sheppard, Giraldez, future)
│   │   └── attributed.yaml    # composed, labeled as such
│   ├── education.yaml
│   └── network.yaml
├── generators/
│   ├── skills-sheet.ts
│   ├── cv-warm.ts
│   ├── cv-serious.ts
│   ├── cv-bairesdev.ts
│   └── landing.ts
├── design-system/
│   ├── tokens.css
│   ├── components.css
│   └── patterns.css
└── dist/                      # generated outputs (PDFs, site)

06 · Phases

Strict order. No parallel scope.

A4 PDF, 2 pp, dual-visual skills map, shareable by email. Populated from /data/skills.yaml.

deliverable · pdf

CV × 3 variants

Warm (LinkedIn), Serious (enterprise), BairesDev (template-safe). Same /data/, three templates.

deliverable · pdfs

DaniloRojas.design

Huly.io aesthetic · tab-SPA pattern · mobile-first responsive. Includes Phase 4 hook for the "me" agent.

deliverable · web

Approval gate between each phase: the next does not start until the previous is generated, approved, and in Danilo's hands.

07 · Positioning rules

  • Role: Agentic Designer · Product Engineer (hybrid headline).
  • Tone: serious + warm. Authoritative without distance.
  • Frame: profile + agentic AI = combination.
  • Language: EN primary, ES toggle.
  • Visual: Huly.io light-adapted, controlled structure + per-section animated visual.
  • CTA: always "Let's work together" · no pricing shown.
  • Honesty: testimonials disclosed (verified vs attributed); skills split (mastered vs learning); EnRegla as "early paying pilots" without numbers.
  • Audience: US enterprise, LATAM SMB, full-time remote, SaaS investor, BairesDev template.
