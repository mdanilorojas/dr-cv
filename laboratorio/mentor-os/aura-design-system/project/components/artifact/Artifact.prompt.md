Artifact is Aura's signature surface — it frames a produced or running AI artifact (a generated CV, a live preview) as first-class product UI. EmbedWell is the file-embed block placed in its body.

```jsx
<Artifact file="cv-serious.en.pdf" meta="generated 14 Jun 2026" status="ready">
  <EmbedWell kind="PDF" title="Danilo Rojas — CV" meta="2 pages · 184 KB" />
</Artifact>
```

Compose with Stream, PlanSteps, DiffReview and SkillChip inside the body to show visible thinking, plan-before-build and diff-review.
