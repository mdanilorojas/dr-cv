Mastery is the skill-level badge used across the diagnostic — its five levels carry sacred semantics; the colors never change meaning.

```jsx
<Mastery level="mastered" />
<Mastery level="gap">Critical gap</Mastery>
```

Levels: `mastered` (green) · `proficient` (blue) · `developing` (amber) · `novice` (grey) · `gap` (red). Defaults its own label from the level; pass children to override.
