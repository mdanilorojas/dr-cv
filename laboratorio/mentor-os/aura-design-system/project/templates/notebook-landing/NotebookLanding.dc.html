// thesis · 01

Most portfolios argue from output — screens, shots, the polished final frame. This one argues from method. Fifteen years of shipping taught me that taste is necessary and no longer sufficient; the differentiated work now lives in how you run the loop.

For the last twelve months I have rebuilt that loop around agents: plan before build, visible thinking, diff-review every change, name the skills I reach for. The artifacts on this page are not screenshots. They are the actual surfaces I work inside, embedded live.

The objective is deliberately a number. $250,000 a year as a Product Designer — because a number can be diagnosed, ranked, and closed. Everything else is decoration.
